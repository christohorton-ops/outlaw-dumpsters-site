Why Outlaw Exists

I'm Chris, born and raised in Utah. I answer the phone when you call. Before starting this business I did three home remodels, and the first two I tried to haul myself. Rented a trailer, drove to the Lindon transfer station, and both times completely underestimated how much a bathroom gut actually weighs. Some lessons you pay twice.

The third time I rented from a national company. They showed up six hours late, set the bin down on bare concrete, and added a fuel surcharge that wasn't anywhere in the original quote. That's when I decided to just run my own operation.

We're licensed and insured in Utah. We put specialized dumpster wheels (rollie skates) under every drop to protect your driveway. And we quote the real price, landfill ticket included, before the truck rolls.

Today we run dumpsters across Utah and Salt Lake Counties for homeowners doing weekend cleanouts, contractors framing new builds in Saratoga Springs, and property managers turning units in Sandy. Every drop is handled by someone who lives within 20 minutes of you.

FAQ

Common Questions

Answered by Chris, owner of Outlaw Dumpsters and a Utah homeowner who's hauled (and rented) his fair share.

Ask Chris Directly
How quickly can you deliver a dumpster in Utah or Salt Lake County?
Same-day delivery is available for most of our service area when you call before noon. We're headquartered in Lehi, so Utah County deliveries are typically our fastest. Salt Lake County is usually next-day but we do same-day there too when we can. Text or call (801) 753-8256 and we'll let you know exactly what to expect.
What size dumpster do I need for a home remodel?
For most Utah home projects, bathroom gut, kitchen remodel, full room cleanout, roofing job, landscaping overhaul, the 15-yard is the right call. In practical terms it's about two or three trips with a full pickup and trailer. If you're on the fence, call and describe what you've got. I'd rather talk you through it than have you rent something too small.
What can't I put in the dumpster?
Batteries, tires, and electronics need specific recycling and can't go in. Mattresses are allowed at $25 per mattress, just give us a heads up. Pure concrete-only or pure dirt-only loads aren't accepted, but mixed loads with some concrete or soil are fine. When in doubt, shoot me a text before you load it up.
How is your pricing structured? Are there hidden fees?
No hidden fees. The $250 flat rate covers delivery, pickup, 5 days of rental, and disposal of up to 2,000 lbs. Additional days are $25 each. Weight overages over 2,000 lbs are 5 cents per pound, and I always call before adding anything to your bill. No surprises after the fact.
Will the dumpster damage my driveway?
We put specialized dumpster wheels (we call them rollie skates) under every drop. They spread the weight out so your concrete or asphalt doesn't take a hit. No plywood needed on your end. It's just how we set up every job.
